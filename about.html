About Me

From Finance to AI: A Journey of Continuous Innovation

My Story

With over 15 years of experience bridging the worlds of finance and technology, I've transformed from a Chartered Accountant (FCCA) into a Data Science innovator who creates intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. My unique blend of qualifications – FCCA, MBA, BSc in Economics, and MSc in Data Science – positions me at the intersection of business strategy and technological innovation.

My journey began in traditional finance, where I mastered the art of turning numbers into insights through roles in audit, financial analysis, and strategic planning. But I saw a bigger picture – a future where AI and machine learning could revolutionize how businesses make decisions. This vision led me to pursue an MSc in Data Science from the University of Salford, where I graduated with distinction, specializing in deep learning, NLP, and predictive analytics.

Today, I architect AI solutions that don't just process data – they predict trends, automate decisions, and create value across industries. From developing financial management platforms for African SMEs to building intelligent visa tracking systems, I'm passionate about using technology to democratize access to advanced analytics. My work spans multiple domains: fintech innovation, educational technology, immigration intelligence, and enterprise automation.

What sets me apart is my ability to speak both languages fluently – the language of business and the language of technology. I don't just build models; I build solutions that CFOs understand, that CEOs can envision, and that transform organizational capabilities. Whether it's implementing fraud detection systems, optimizing supply chains with ML, or creating AI-powered customer insights, I bring a practitioner's precision and a strategist's vision to every project.
