About Me

From Finance to AI: A Journey of Continuous Innovation

My Story

With over 15 years of experience bridging the worlds of finance and technology, I've transformed from a Fellow Chartered Certified Accountant (FCCA) into a Data Science innovator who creates intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. My unique blend of qualifications – FCCA, MBA Finance, BSc Administration (Accounting) First Class Honours, and MSc Data Science (in progress) – positions me at the intersection of business strategy and technological innovation.

My journey began in traditional finance, where I mastered the art of turning numbers into insights through roles spanning treasury management, financial reporting, and strategic analytics. From managing $300M+ Letter of Credit operations to leading accounting operations across multiple entities with zero audit discrepancies, I built a solid foundation in financial excellence. But I saw a bigger picture – a future where AI and machine learning could revolutionize business decisions.

This vision led me to pursue an MSc in Data Science at Middlesex University, London, where I am currently advancing my expertise in Machine Learning, Big Data, Visual Data Analysis, Cloud Systems, NLP, and AI Ethics. My transition has been marked by practical achievements: building Power BI dashboards that improved reporting efficiency by 35%, automating processes that reduced manual work by 50%, and developing predictive models that enhanced decision-making by 20%.

Today, I architect data science solutions that bridge the gap between complex analytics and business value. My projects span from London Airbnb sentiment analysis using NLP to diabetes risk classification with machine learning. I'm passionate about applying data science to fintech innovation, healthcare AI, and ESG initiatives. What sets me apart is my ability to combine deep financial domain expertise with technical proficiency in Python, SQL, cloud platforms, and advanced analytics tools.
